Welcome

I'm Jules, a licensed therapist who loves to teach individuals how to heal from stress, trauma, and disruptive patterns which keep them stuck. Life is constantly trying to move us forward, which sometimes makes us feel as though our world is falling apart. Through individual psychotherapy and coaching, I support clients with developing a new world, one that feels better and one that they can be proud of.

About

As a kid, my mom took me to a therapist to figure out why I was skipping school. The therapist looked at me like I was an alien. I felt like an alien. I promised myself that if I ever got the chance to help someone, I wouldn't do that.

In 2018, I created Hope+Healing+Happiness Life Solutions, a private practice in Greenville, South Carolina which offers in-person and virtual holistic psychotherapy services. I facilitate therapy from a trauma-informed, anti-oppressive perspective which incorporates an eclectic approach including: psychodynamic, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness-based practices, somatic (body-based) and EMDR (eye movement desensitization reprocessing).

I work with adults dealing with a variety of issues including, trauma/PTSD, depression, anxiety, stress, racial and gender discrimination, relationships challenges, life transitions. I proudly work with communities of color, as well as individuals within the LGBTQIA+ community.

Regardless of how you identify or what you are experiencing, my space is always a safe space for you.

where hope, healing, and happiness thrives!
